Dysis JT-1

Single-Seat Recreational / Convertible Trainer — Jet Powered

In Design

The JT-1 is a single-seat conceptual jet aircraft designed for recreational flight and adaptable as a two-seat trainer configuration. Powered by a twin-spool low-bypass turbofan, it balances agility with efficiency across a wide flight envelope.

Dysis PT-1

Single-Seat Recreational / Convertible Trainer — Piston Powered

In Design

The PT-1 is a single-seat conceptual piston-engine aircraft designed for recreational use with an option for two-seat trainer conversion. Its high-displacement supercharged and turbocharged engine delivers exceptional power output while a liquid-cooled system with intercooler and radiator ensures sustained high-altitude performance.
